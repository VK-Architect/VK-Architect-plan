Why VK Architect

Well before I decided to learn coding I wanted to be a pilot. That didn't work out as planned, because my marks didn't allow for me to get a bursary and flights school is very expensive more than 1mil. I was lost didn't know what to do, but then I was reminded of coding.

Now I chose coding because I discovered that I could become a self-taught web developer. I tried it to see if my interest would fade. You know what? It didn't .

Even though it can be one of the most irritating things in my life especially since I’m pursuing it on a phone it’s still something I care about. Maybe not more irritating than Clash Royale, but it’s definitely up there.
