The Plan

What is the VK Architect plan? It is a plan for my success, a plan that shows my determination.

Right now, I’m lost because I don’t know what I want to be. My matric marks are not enough to support my dream of becoming a pilot.

Because of that, I am on a journey to discover a new path through coding. It’s hard, especially because I don’t have a laptop or a PC to make things easier. This is not by choice, but by circumstance.

That won’t stop me, though. I’m currently using my phone to learn web design. Web design itself isn’t that hard, but doing it on a phone is extremely difficult and requires a lot of patience — especially on Huawei, at least for me.

Why VK Architect

When I started this coding journey, I was lost. I didn’t know what to do, and because of my marks, I was unsure whether coding was even an option for me. Still, I decided to go for it.

I chose coding because I discovered that I could become a self-taught web developer. I told myself to try it and see whether I would enjoy it. And you know what? I actually do.

Even though it can be one of the most irritating things in my life — especially since I’m pursuing it on a phone — it’s still something I care about. Maybe not more irritating than Clash Royale, but it’s definitely up there.

The Scaffold

Although I would like to say I’m doing everything on my own, I can’t. No one builds something meaningful alone, and I’ve had help from my trusted Scaffold.

Just like every builder needs a scaffold while constructing a building, I also need my Scaffold while I’m still in the early stages of building my future.
